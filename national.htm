Collapse of Kamilang’ombe Bridge

A newly constructed bridge in Chitipa partially collapsed leaving a vehicle hanging...

In T/A Nthalire, Chitipa, a newly built Kamilang’ombe Bridge partially collapsed yesterday. A Toyota Land Cruiser carrying People’s Party members was trapped mid-bridge. Locals quickly used logs and sand to rescue the passengers, miraculously with no serious injuries. The bridge, constructed at K167 million last month, had visible cracks. Chitipa Council has ordered the contractor to redo the project. This incident raises concerns about infrastructure safety ahead of the general elections.

Malawi Ranked 71st in Global Peace Index 2025

Malawi ranks among the top 43% most peaceful countries in the world...

The 2025 Global Peace Index ranks Malawi 71st worldwide. This places Malawi ahead of major tourist destinations like France, USA, and Thailand in terms of safety and stability. Within Africa, Malawi ranks in the top 20% most peaceful nations. While global conflicts increase, Malawi remains relatively stable, earning its reputation as "The Warm Heart of Africa."
